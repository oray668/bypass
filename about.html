Bypass Journal is an ongoing collaboration between a group of recent architectural graduates that maintains a platform for exploring, documenting, sharing and advocating for alternative modes of thought about the practice, discipline and agency of architecture. We are interested in working with what we already have, thinking through drawing, finding opportunity in failure, researching through making, slowing down and being inclusive in process and outcome: how can we practice architecture on an overburdened planet?
